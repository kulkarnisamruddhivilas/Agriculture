Types of Farming

Explore Different Farming Methods

Subsistence Farming

Farmers grow crops mainly for their own consumption. It is common in rural areas.

Commercial Farming

Large-scale farming done to sell crops in the market and earn profit.

Organic Farming

Uses natural fertilizers and avoids chemicals to protect environment and health.

Mixed Farming

Combination of crop production and livestock farming.

Intensive Farming

High use of labor and fertilizers to increase production from small land.
